Our Branches

Colombo 6

Located in the heart of Sri Lanka's bustling capital, our Colombo branch stands as a beacon of quality and freshness. Conveniently situated to serve both busy professionals and families, this store offers a wide selection of vegetables and fruits sourced daily from local farmers. Our dairy section boasts the finest products, from fresh milk to artisanal cheeses, ensuring that our customers enjoy the best of local dairy. Additionally, our cosmetics and skincare range features top-notch, eco-friendly brands, catering to the health-conscious urbanite. With its modern layout and friendly staff, the Colombo branch promises a delightful shopping experience in a vibrant urban setting.

Kandy

Nestled in the historic city of Kandy, our branch offers a unique blend of tradition and modernity. This store is a go-to destination for residents and visitors seeking fresh produce, with a focus on organic and locally-sourced vegetables and fruits. Our dairy aisle is stocked with high-quality products, perfect for making traditional Sri Lankan dishes. The cosmetics and skincare section showcases a curated selection of products, ideal for those looking to pamper themselves amidst the hustle and bustle of Kandy. With its serene ambiance and commitment to quality, the Kandy branch ensures a pleasant and fulfilling shopping experience

Galle

Situated in the picturesque coastal city of Galle, our branch provides a refreshing shopping experience with a scenic touch. Known for its fresh, sun-kissed fruits and vegetables, this store is a favorite among locals and tourists alike. Our dairy section is filled with premium products, catering to a variety of tastes and dietary needs. The cosmetics and skincare range here includes both popular and niche brands, emphasizing natural and sustainable ingredients. The Galle branch, with its welcoming environment and attentive service, offers a delightful retreat for shoppers looking to enjoy the best in fresh produce and personal care items by the sea.
